Overview of the user interface

### Dashboard:
- Upon logging in, you will be presented with the dashboard, which serves as the central hub for managing your cases and tasks.
- The dashboard provides an overview of your pending tasks, active cases, and other relevant information.
### Navigation Bar:
- The navigation bar is located at the top of the application and remains visible as you navigate.
- It contains essential navigation elements such as:
    - Home: Takes you back to the dashboard from any section of the application.
    - Cases: Allows you to view and manage your cases.
    - Tasks: Provides access to your assigned tasks and task lists.
    - Reports: Allows you to generate and view reports on case performance and analytics.
    - Administration: Provides access to administrative settings and configuration options.
    - Help: Offers assistance and support options.
### Sidebar Menu:
- The sidebar menu is typically located on the left side of the application.
- It presents a hierarchical list of menu items that allow you to navigate to various sections and features of the application.
- Each menu item may have sub-items to further organize and categorize functionality.
### Case Management:
- The case management section allows you to create, view, and manage cases.
- You can search for existing cases, create new cases, assign cases to users or teams, and track case progress.
- Within each case, you can access case details, documents, related tasks, and case history.
### Task Management:
- The task management section displays your assigned tasks and task lists.
- You can view and prioritize your tasks, update task status, and collaborate with other users on task-related activities.
- The task management section provides filtering and sorting options to help you organize and find tasks efficiently.
### Reports and Analytics:
- The reports and analytics section allows you to generate and view reports on case performance, process efficiency, and other relevant metrics.
- You can choose from pre-defined report templates or create custom reports based on your specific requirements.
### Administration:
- The administration section is accessible to authorized users responsible for configuring and managing the WKS Platform.
- It provides options to define case types, configure workflows, manage user roles and permissions, and customize the application's behavior.
### Search Functionality:
- The application includes a powerful search feature that allows you to find specific cases, tasks, or process instances quickly.
- Look for a search bar or input field where you can enter keywords or filters to retrieve relevant results.
### Notifications and Alerts:
- The application may display notifications or alerts to inform you about important updates, new tasks, or system events.
- These notifications are often represented by icons or badges in the user interface, notifying you of unread items.
### Help and Support:
- If you encounter any issues or need assistance, the application provides a help or support section.
- This section may include a knowledge base, FAQs, documentation, or a contact form to reach out to the support team.
